Bagels

Description

A recipe for making bagels. Taken from Kibitz Spot.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups lukewarm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar (okay to substitute white sugar)
  • 1 Tablespoon yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive, canola, or other light oil
  • 4 cups white flour

Directions

  1. Mix water, salt, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Mix until it forms a single dough ball.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Measure out 100 grams per bagel and form into rings by rolling in your palm and connecting the ends.
  6. Place bagel rings on a baking paper-lined baking sheet. Let rise for 40-50 minutes.
  7. Boil bagel rings one minute on each side. (We boil two at a time and use a flat spatula to flip them in the water and remove them from the pot.)
  8. Place on a wire rack to allow water to drip off (about 5 minutes).
  9. Place the boiled bagel on an oiled baking sheet (okay to use baking paper instead of oil).
  10. Bake 20 minutes at 350°F (180°C) or until golden brown.
